State Tax Extensions 2008
Completing and filing a tax extension form will give you additional time to complete your 2008 tax return – in most cases you can get an extension until August 15, 2009. You are still required to pay any taxes due by April 15, 2009 or you may be subject to penalties and interest.From the experts at TaxBrain:
