Chain stitch.
is a and technique in which a series of looped stitches form a -like pattern. Chain stitch is an ancient craft - examples of surviving chain stitch embroidery worked in have been dated to the (5th-3rd century BC). Handmade chain stitch embroidery does not require that the needle pass through more than one layer of fabric. For this reason the stitch is an effective surface embellishment near seams on finished fabric.
