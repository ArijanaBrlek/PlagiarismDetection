Connecticut Contractor Subcontractor Agreement
This form is intended to be used by an independent contractor to hire a subcontractor to perform certain work on a job that the contractor has agreed to perform. It provides for the armingout of specific work to be performed by the subcontractor within a set time period for a particular payment. It also provides a method for authorizing extra work under the contract. Finally, this document provides that the subcontractor agrees to indemnify the contractor against any claims or liabilities arising from the performance of the work. This form is for use in Connecticut. Labors and Materials Work Schedule Professionalism Extra Services Modification of Agreement
