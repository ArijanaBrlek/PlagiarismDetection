Graduate Studies & Non-traditional Programs
Connecticut College is known for a demanding academic program that also allows lots of room for creativity, self-expression and personal growth. The College offers graduate study and non-traditional programs to support lifelong learning.
Graduate Studies
The College grants the Master of Arts programs in biological sciences, botany, chemistry, music, and psychology. You must be a graduate in good standing from an accredited college or university. Properly qualified candidates are admitted to graduate study following approval of the Graduate Studies Committee and of the department concerned.
Please Note: The MAT (Master of Arts in Teaching) has been discontinued for the foreseeable future. There is no timeline as to when or if it will be reinstated.
