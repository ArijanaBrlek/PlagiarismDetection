EyeToy USB Camera (for PlayStation 2)
Be part of the game with PlayStation's revolutionary EyeToy camera peripheral for PlayStation 2. Place this cute little device on top of your TV, plug it into one of the PlayStation 2 console's USB ports, and get playing - it's incredibly simple to use and guaranteed to get any party started!
Once connected, this multi-purpose device can be used in conjunction with compatible PlayStation titles in a variety of innovative ways. Move your body to control the on-screen action, capture a 3D model of your head and become the star of the game, create photographs and videos of yourself and your friends, and much, much more. With the EyeToy: Chat application (sold separately), you can even use EyeToy and its in-built microphone as a videophone!
