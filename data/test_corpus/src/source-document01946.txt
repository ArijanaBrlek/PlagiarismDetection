Alexian Brothers Values
We manifest God's presence in the world by a concerned approach and competent care of those we serve.Compassion.
Because we believe that all individuals are created in God's image. Therefore, we treat them with respect.Dignity of the Person.
We serve the sick, aged, dying and unwanted of all socio-economic levels, especially the poor.Care of the Poor.
We promote healing of the whole person (body, mind and spirit) through physical, psycho-social and spiritual care of the whole person.Holism.
We collaborate with those who share our ministry in carrying out Our mission.Partnership.
