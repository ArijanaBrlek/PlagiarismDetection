Cooking London Broil in Slow Cooker
Cooking london broil in slow cooker makes a flavorful cut of meat when carved diagonally across the grain into thin slices. Marinating it makes it tender.
Easy
Seasonings to use onion salt, pepper, garlic
Brown flank steak in frying pan. Put in crock pot.
Season with seasonings.
Cook: Low 8 to 10 hours
Then slice as directed above.
Flavorful
1 1/2 lbs. flank steak
1 1/2 T. vegetable oil
Salt
Rub garlic clove on both sides vigorously. Rub with oil.
Put in crock pot.
Cook: Low 8 to 10 hours
Then slice.
