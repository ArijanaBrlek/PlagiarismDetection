RV & Marine
We offer Renewable solar energy solutions for RV and Marine applications. RV and Marine Solar power systems are an ideal alternative to expensive and dirty fuels. Affordable Solar offers all the products you will need to build a complete RV or Marine Solar Powered System. Whether you are looking for just a solar panel or inverter, or a complete Solar Kit, you will find it here. Solar electricity is the perfect solution for remote situations such as on a boat or in an RV without ready access to a power outlet. You can store your electricity generated from your solar panels in a battery for use anytime, or directly power your electrical devices while the sun is out. Solar and wind electric systems are a great way to charge a large battery bank in an RV or a boat.
