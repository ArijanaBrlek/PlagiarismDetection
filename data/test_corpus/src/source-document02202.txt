Triglycerides
What are triglycerides? They are a type of fats that are essential for a good health especially when they are in normal levels. They are present in some foods we eat and are also made by the body. They account for 95% of the body tissues. They are chains of high energy fatty acids that supply a lot of energy to our cells so they can work at an optimal level.
