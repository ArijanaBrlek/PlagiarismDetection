About Fun and Fitness for Children at My Gym Bellevue
Table of contents
Play, Learn and Explore at My Gym has been set up to bring moms and young children together in a safe, clean and fun environment.Whether you want to join a weekly class or just come to special events, there is always something going on at My Gym. Special events include: Waddlers Parties (sponsored by Huggies) Open Gym Storytime Pizza Parties/Ice cream socials Open Houses Parents Night Out Classes and many of the events do have a fee, however, some events are free and we also offer group events for smaller fees. We are always willing to add new new classes, events, lectures and special activities based on the interests of our community.
