Disney Mania
Main Street, U.S.A. is where Disneyland guests find themselves magically teleported to a turn-of-the-century mid-West town, bustling and alive with friendship, hard work and much play. Amongst the Victorian buildings painted in a vast array of pastels and other colors pleasing to the eye can be heard the constant "clop-clopping" of horse's hooves as they make their way up and down the busy street. The scent of caramel-dipped apples and freshly-made ice cream cones accompany the sounds of the local barbershop quartet and the always-grinning pianist playing his heart out here in this delicious slice of yesteryear.
