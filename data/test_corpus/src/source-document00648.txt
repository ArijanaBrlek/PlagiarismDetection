Slim Internet Media USB Keyboard - AKB04US
The Slim Internet Media USB Keyboard has all the functionality of a standard keyboard with the addition of built-in Internet and Multimedia hot keys. It features the latest keycap technology to reduce the key travel distance to less than 50% of the traditional keyboards. The slim keyboard is comfortable and reduces tension to the wrists and arms, as well as finger fatigue when compared to using a standard keyboard. It takes up little space on your desktop and is aesthetically a better match to LCD monitors and notebook PCs.
