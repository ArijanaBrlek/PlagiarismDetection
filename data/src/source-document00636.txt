The Center for Capacity Development - We help organizations succeed
In 2002, the WorkPlace, Inc. ( Workforce Development Board) launched
Southwestern Connecticut
  The Center for Capacity Development , a fee-for-service initiative aimed at building the financial, programmatic and organizational capacity of not-for-profit organizations, community-based organizations, faith-based organizations, small businesses and governmental agencies to identify, secure, and manage funding from a variety of sources.
