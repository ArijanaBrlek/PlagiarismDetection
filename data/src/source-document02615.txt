YOUR GUIDE TO THE SUBSURFACE
Geomap Company's Geologic Mapping Services provide the explorationist with a complete and carefully updated set of multiple horizon structure maps in either CD-ROM or hardcopy format. Geomap boasts of one of the finest collections of historical well logs in the United States and is constantly acquiring newly released logs. Our team of geologists revise and update the service areas and Geomap subscribers receive a complete set of maps on a regular schedule. For more detailed information and contour horizons, please fill out our guest book.
Geomap Base Map Divisions are available for browsing. Most well spotting is current to within 120 days. Contact us for the latest information on revision dates for your area of interest.
