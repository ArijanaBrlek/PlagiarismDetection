I got my baking stone about eight years ago and still use it constantly. I don't make a lot of pizzas, but cookies turn out perfectly every time. There are some great recipes in the instruction book as well(the fruit pizza is wonderful). The newer ones come with handles which must be very helpful because the stone itself is a little awkward to get in and out of the oven. It does get very hot and takes some time to cool down.
