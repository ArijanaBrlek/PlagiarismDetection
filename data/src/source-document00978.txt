Buzz! Buzzers
The Buzz! Buzzers are a fantastic new way to play a whole host of great games on PlayStation 2  anyone can join in.
Whether you're enjoying one of the Buzz! Quiz games (live in your own living room) or competing in party games like the Buzz! Junior range of titles, the Buzz! Buzzers are simple to use and great fun for everyone. Up to four players can compete at once with one set of Buzz! Buzzers, so you play on your own or challenge friends and family to some frantic buzzing action. It's easy to set up the Buzz! Buzzers - just insert the USB connector into either USB connector on the front of the PlayStation 2 console.
