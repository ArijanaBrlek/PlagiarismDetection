Overview
At Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, scientific breakthroughs happen everyday. So does being a good neighbor. From cultivating relationships with our community to building advocacy within the region, the Community and Regional Outreach staff provides the resources and programs to ensure that PNNL is an asset to the northwest region and the local community.
The Community and Regional Outreach organization builds mutually beneficial relationships and implements strategies to promote economic vitality, generate goodwill and advocacy for the laboratory, the community and our regional neighbors.
These relationships with key policy makers and opinion leaders foster the laboratory's reputation not only as a vital science and technology resource but also as a vital .neighbor
